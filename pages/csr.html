What do we do?

We support our client’s corporate social responsibility initiatives by refurbishing near end-of-life electronic devices, for example, laptop and desktop computers, servers and more for re-use by clients, or donation to staff and charities as part of their corporate social responsibility.

This Service guarantees the following

Access to affordable refurbished electrical and electronic products for companies, employees and private individuals

CSR opportunity for companies and private individuals to donate affordable refurbished equipment to staff members or charity

Issuance of eco-friendly certificate of compliance to CSR clients.
